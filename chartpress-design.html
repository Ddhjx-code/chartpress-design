Before you press

Are the measurements actually accurate?+
Yes. Every blank's specs come from the manufacturer's published size chart (Gildan, Bella+Canvas, Comfort Colors, etc.), entered as flat garment measurements. We flag the ±0.5" production tolerance on every chart so your buyers know what to expect.
What if my blank isn't in the library?+
We're launching with the 8 most popular blanks. If yours isn't here yet, request it and we'll add it within days. Custom blank entry is next on the roadmap once we validate demand.
Which marketplaces do the exports fit?+
PNG exports are pre-sized for Etsy listing images, Amazon product images, and TikTok Shop. The HTML snippet drops into Shopify product descriptions and any custom storefront.
Is it really free?+
Really. Every theme, every export, every blank — free. No account, no watermark, no upsell hiding the good stuff. We're testing whether sellers actually want this; if it's useful, tell another seller. That's the whole deal.
Will this actually reduce my returns?+
Size is the #1 reason apparel gets returned. A clear, accurate chart in your listing images sets correct expectations before purchase. Sellers consistently report a meaningful drop in "doesn't fit" returns after adding one — that's the whole point of this tool.
